Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some common questions our customers ask about orders, customization, delivery, and more. If you need further help, feel free to contact us anytime.

How can I place an order?

You can place an order by contacting us through WhatsApp or Instagram. Share your product choice, customization ideas, and delivery details—we’ll take care of the rest.

Do you take customized gift orders?

Yes, all our products can be customized based on your occasion and preferences—names, photos, colors, themes, and more.

What is the delivery time?

Delivery usually takes 4–7 working days, depending on your location and the complexity of the customization.

Do you offer bulk or return gifts?

Yes! We handle both individual and bulk orders for return gifts, corporate events, and special functions with special discounts.

Can I see a sample before final delivery?

Yes, we usually send a preview or sample photo for confirmation before packing and shipping the final product.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept UPI, bank transfers, and payment links. A partial or full advance payment is required to begin processing.
